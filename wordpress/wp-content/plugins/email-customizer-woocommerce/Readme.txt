== Description ==
Email Customizer WooCommerce is a add-on for WooCommerce to keep your customer updated with their product delivery status. The existing feature of WooCommerce product tracking with email updates to customer have been enhanced to notify with their product delivery. The add-on adds a custom status “Product Delivered” with customizable email template. You can change the template in the way you want it adorn. 

= Custom Templates =
* Craft unique email template for New user registration, reset password and customer note.
* Preview the email template before it reaches your customer’s inbox.
* Simple drag & drop to design email templates.

Stay close with your customers by keeping them in touch on each stages of your product delivery process.

= Enhanced Premium Version =
* The Premium version has all the features of the Free version.
* Send Product based and category based emails.
* Embed digital signature in each WooCommerce order emails.  
* Send test emails to take a look at how the emails are delivered in customer mailbox.
* Craft your own subject lines.
* The layout of the default and custom status “Product Delivery” template can be customized.

== Installation ==
WooCommerce Email Customizer can be installed in usual 3 methods

I. For simple general way to install

* Go to Plugins → Add New → Upload
* Browse and Upload the downloaded zip file
* Activate the plugin.

II. For familiar FTP users

* Extract the plugin zip
* Upload plugin zip to /wp-content/plugins/
* Go to Plugins → Installed Plugins → Active 	

III. Straight from WordPress Admin

* Go to plugins → Add New
* Search for Email Customizer WooCommerce
* Click Install Now → Activate
